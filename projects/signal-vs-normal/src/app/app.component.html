Angular 19 · OnPush Change Detection

Signal vs Normal Variable

See exactly why changing a normal variable does nothing in OnPush, while a Signal always keeps the UI in sync.

🚫
Normal Variable + OnPush
Angular skips re-rendering unless an @Input changes, an event fires, or an async pipe emits.
→
✅
Signal + OnPush
Angular tracks every signal read. Any .set() or .update() automatically schedules a precise re-render.
